About Us

Redefining Mobility for Billions RideNow is India’s largest mobility platform . The RideNow app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across, metered taxis, and cabs, enabling convenience and transparency for hundreds of millions of consumers and over 1.5 million driver-partners.
RideNow is pne pf the safest and time saving online cab booking website, that aims to enable millions of livelihood opportunities for India's youth. With its acquisition of Ridlr, India’s leading public transportation app and investment in Vogo, a dockless scooter sharing solution, RideNow is looking to build mobility for the next billion Indians. RideNow also extends its consumer offerings like micro-insurance and credit led payments through RideNow Financial Services and a range of owned food brands through India’s largest network of kitchens under its Food business.


RideNow was founded in September 2022 by TEAM BRURE FORCE with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
